Why Convert JPG to PNG?

🔍

Lossless Quality

PNG uses lossless compression, meaning no quality is lost during conversion. Perfect for logos and graphics.

🎨

Transparent Background

PNG supports transparency (alpha channel), unlike JPG. Convert to PNG when you need a transparent background.

📐

Sharp Edges

PNG is better for images with sharp edges, text, or flat colors — ideal for UI screenshots and graphics.

🔒

Frequently Asked Questions

Is JPG to PNG conversion free?

Yes, completely free. SwiftImage converts JPG to PNG with no cost, no registration, and no file size limits.

Does converting JPG to PNG improve quality?

The conversion itself doesn't improve existing quality, but PNG's lossless format prevents further quality loss from re-saving.

Will my images be uploaded to a server?

No. Everything happens locally in your browser using HTML5 technology. Your images are 100% private.

Can I convert multiple JPG files at once?

Yes! Select multiple JPG files at once and convert them all to PNG in one click.

What is the difference between JPG and PNG?

JPG uses lossy compression (smaller size, some quality loss). PNG uses lossless compression (larger size, no quality loss, supports transparency).
